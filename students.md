## Research Opportunities

I welcome motivated students at all levels (undergraduate, master's, and PhD) to join my research group. We work on cutting-edge problems in software engineering, with a focus on data-driven approaches and empirical methods.

### Current Openings

**PhD Positions**
- **Research Areas**: Software security, mining software repositories, developer analytics, blockchain security
- **Requirements**: Strong background in computer science, programming skills, research experience preferred
- **Funding**: Full scholarships available for qualified candidates
- **Start Date**: Fall and Spring admissions

**Master's Research Projects**
- **Duration**: 1-2 years for thesis research
- **Topics**: Various projects aligned with current research directions
- **Opportunities**: Conference presentations, publications, industry collaborations

**Undergraduate Research**
- **Programs**: Summer research internships, final year projects, independent study
- **Time Commitment**: Flexible based on student availability
- **Outcomes**: Research experience, potential publications, recommendation letters

## Application Process

### For PhD Applicants

1. **Initial Contact**: Send CV and research statement to my email
2. **Background Match**: Review of academic background and research interests
3. **Interview**: Discussion of research ideas and fit
4. **Formal Application**: Submission through university admissions portal
5. **Funding Consideration**: Scholarship and assistantship opportunities

### For Master's Applicants

1. **Project Discussion**: Identify research project of mutual interest
2. **Proposal Development**: Help in developing research proposal
3. **Supervision Agreement**: Formal arrangement through department
4. **Registration**: Course registration and research credit setup

### For Undergraduate Researchers

1. **Expression of Interest**: Contact with brief background and interests
2. **Project Matching**: Identify suitable project based on skills and time
3. **Initial Meeting**: Discussion of expectations and deliverables
4. **Formal Enrollment**: Through relevant programs or independent study
