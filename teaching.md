## Teaching Philosophy

I believe in creating an interactive and engaging learning environment where students can develop both theoretical understanding and practical skills in software engineering. My teaching approach emphasizes:

- **Hands-on Experience**: Connecting theoretical concepts with real-world applications
- **Critical Thinking**: Encouraging students to analyze, evaluate, and create
- **Collaborative Learning**: Fostering teamwork and peer learning
- **Research Integration**: Connecting teaching with current research trends
